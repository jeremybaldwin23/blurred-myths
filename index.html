Blurred Myths

Reexamining the reputations of ancient figures.

Purpose

Through Blurred Myths, I aim to explore the gray area between good and evil in ancient mythology. By looking at and picking apart figures like Medusa, Zeus, and Odysseus, this project challenges the modern reputations we assign to these characters. Are these figures truly villainous? Are they truly heroes?
